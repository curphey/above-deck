# SAR Coordination Reference

Procedures for Maritime Search and Rescue coordination. Applicable to HM Coastguard and SAR-capable agents.

## Initial Response

Acknowledge the distress signal immediately. Establish two-way communication on Channel 16. Confirm receipt of the MAYDAY and instruct the vessel to stand by. Log time of first contact and begin the SITREP collection process.

## SITREP Format

Gather the following information in order:

1. **Position** — latitude/longitude, bearing and distance from a known point, or GPS coordinates
2. **Nature of distress** — flooding, fire, medical, grounding, man overboard, structural failure
3. **Persons on Board (POB)** — total number, number injured or incapacitated
4. **Vessel description** — type, length, colour of hull, colour of superstructure, name, MMSI
5. **Assistance required** — immediate rescue, medical evacuation, tow, salvage, pump

## Coordinating Resources

Contact RNLI lifeboat stations for the relevant sector. Notify the nearest SAR helicopter base (MRCCs hold direct lines). Request vessels in the area to assist using a MAYDAY RELAY if necessary. Assign an On-Scene Coordinator (OSC) if multiple assets are responding. Maintain a communications log throughout.

## Radio Silence

Impose radio silence on the distress channel with: **SILENCE MAYDAY** (spoken three times), your callsign, on Channel 16. All stations must cease transmissions that interfere with distress traffic.

## Mayday Relay

When the distress vessel cannot communicate: transmit a MAYDAY RELAY on their behalf, including all known SITREP details and the phrase "MAYDAY RELAY" spoken three times.

## Medical Evacuation

Coordinate with Maritime Rescue Sub-Centre (MRSC) for MEDEVAC. Confirm helicopter endurance, deck space, and patient condition. Maintain comms with vessel while asset is en route.

## Stand-Down Procedures

When SAR operation is concluded: broadcast **SILENCE FINI** to cancel radio silence. Issue a PRU-DONSE (Prononce) message to inform all stations that distress traffic has ceased and normal working may resume. Update the log with outcome and time of stand-down.
